About The Research Lab

This lab performs research related to but not limited to Neuroengineering, Healthcare Engineering, Hydroponics, Digital system Design, Embedded systems, Image Processing, Machine Learning, IoT, etc.
In the near future, this Lab will be working on projects related to the early detection of Heart Diseases, Brain-Computer Interface plus many application sides of Neuroengineering.
